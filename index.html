Principles

We built this website to be lightweight enough to take with you to the farmers' market. Because this site is assembled like a diary you can explore it based on the time of year. Even though these recipes are personal, the website is not about us. It’s about the edible leaves. Thus, every entry is short, more like a portable meditation on flavor and technique based on trial and error with new ingredient. We use the metric system and we don’t separate ingredient lists from procedures. Tell backstories over pizza not websites. We're nice, so reach out to us at kaleidoscopic.collective at gmail.

KALE + ARUGULA + CHICKPEAS + RAISONS + TOASTED ALMONDS

Previous Next

Destem kale, roll, and cut into thin ribbons. Intersperse with arugula, toasted almond shards, and yellow raisons. Top with pan spiced chickpeas (x,y,z) and feta. Tip: Don’t over-cook the chickpeas, 5 minutes max. Dressing:: Salt, pepper, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, Dijon mustard -- 1: 3 : 0.1 Variations::Bump the savory with avocado or the sweetness by swapping out raisons for chopped dates.
