Principles

Kale-idoscope is lightweight enough to take with you to the farmers' market. Assembled like a diary you can explore it seasonly. It’s all about the edible leaves, so every entry is a short portable meditation on flavor and technique -- based on trial and error with new ingredients. We promise to use the metric system and combine the ingredient suggestions with the kitchen protocols. Kale-idoscope is all greens forward, so you won't find excess verbage or backstories here. Reach out to us at kaleidoscopic.collective at gmail.

KALE + ARUGULA + CHICKPEAS + RAISONS + TOASTED ALMONDS

Previous Next

Destem kale, roll, and cut into thin ribbons. Intersperse with arugula, toasted almond shards, and yellow raisons. Top with pan spiced chickpeas (x,y,z) and feta. Tip: Don’t over-cook the chickpeas, 5 minutes max. Dressing:: Salt, pepper, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, Dijon mustard -- 1: 3 : 0.1 Variations::Bump the savory with avocado or the sweetness by swapping out raisons for chopped dates.
