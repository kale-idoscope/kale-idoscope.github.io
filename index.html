Kale

Previous Next

Start by rinsing and drying a bunch of kale and arugula, then tear the leaves into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large bowl. Drain and rinse a 15oz can of chickpeas, then add them to the greens. Make a simple vinaigrette by whisking together 3 tablespoons olive oil, 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard, 1 minced garlic clove, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat everything evenly. Top with crumbled feta or shaved Parmesan cheese for extra flavor and protein. You can also add diced tomatoes, sliced red onion, or toasted nuts for crunch.
